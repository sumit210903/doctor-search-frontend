Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How can I book an appointment?

Simply go to the "Book" page, select your doctor and preferred time, and submit the form. You'll receive a confirmation shortly.

❓ How do I edit my profile?

Go to "My Account" and click the Edit Profile button. After updating, you'll be redirected to your profile with changes saved.

❓ Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard security protocols and do not share your personal information with third parties.

❓ Can I cancel an appointment?

Yes, please contact our support or your doctor directly to cancel or reschedule any appointments.
